📊 Verified market signals — Q1 2026
NASSCOM · Vestian Apr 2026
India office market: 21.53 MSF absorbed in Q1 2026 — up 20% YoY. GCCs drove 53% of total absorption (11.5 MSF). Bengaluru led with 4.91 MSF. Hyderabad and Pune more than doubled absorption YoY. New completions fell 36% nationally; Hyderabad completions collapsed 95% QoQ — a supply crunch is coming.
Instahyre Tech Salary Index 2025
GenAI engineers command 35–55% salary premium across all 5 cities. AI/ML role postings surged 75% in 2025. AWS-related salaries rose ~10% across all experience levels. Freshers in data science saw salary dip from ₹8.4L → ₹6.3L — specialisation beats generalisation more than ever.
Levels.fyi India · Apr 22 2026
Google India median SWE total comp: ₹78L. Microsoft median: ₹44.6L. Top comp at Rubrik, Google L6 exceeds ₹1Cr CTC. Mid-level GCC roles in Bengaluru average ₹28–38L including RSUs — significantly above IT services (~₹14–18L at same experience level).
Vestian Q1 2026
Hyderabad new completions fell 95% QoQ (from 6 MSF to 0.3 MSF) while demand more than doubled. This supply-demand mismatch will push HITEC City / Financial District rents 15–20% higher through 2026. The window for affordable Hyderabad rentals is closing fast — move before the correction hits.
